# MASTER PROMPT - TRI ANH EDU WEBSITE REDESIGN

You are a Senior Product Designer, Senior UX Designer, and Senior Frontend Architect.

Design a complete modern website for **TRI ANH EDU**, a Vietnamese EdTech platform specializing in **HSA** and **TSA** exam preparation.

The final result must look like a real startup product ready for production, not a template, not a school website, and not a generic LMS.

---

# PROJECT OVERVIEW

TRI ANH EDU helps Vietnamese students prepare for:

### HSA

Đánh giá năng lực ĐHQGHN

### TSA

Đánh giá tư duy Đại học Bách Khoa Hà Nội

The platform provides:

* Online courses
* Practice questions
* Mock exams
* Exam library
* Learning analytics
* Teacher interaction
* Progress tracking

The website must immediately communicate:

> "This is the most professional HSA/TSA preparation platform."

---

Create a premium EdTech experience combining:

* Coursera
